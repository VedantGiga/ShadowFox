About Us

Founded in 2013, Tanjore Coffee brings you the finest South Indian coffee experience. Our founder, Shri C. Saminathan, with over 35 years of experience in the FMCG industry, has perfected the art of coffee making.

At Tanjore Coffee, we believe in authenticity and quality. Our coffee beans are carefully selected from the finest plantations in South India, ensuring a rich and aromatic brew that captures the essence of traditional South Indian coffee.

What sets us apart is our unique blend of coffee beans, carefully roasted to perfection, and our traditional brewing method that has been passed down through generations. Each cup of Tanjore Coffee is a testament to our commitment to excellence.

We take pride in offering our customers the authentic taste of South Indian filter coffee, whether it's our premium filter coffee powder, instant coffee, or ready-to-drink options. Our products are crafted with the same dedication and expertise that has made South Indian coffee famous worldwide.
